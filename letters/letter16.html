The sixteenth letter

I have a clock standing on the desk. It's digital so it blinks.

It blinks with agony, and hatred, and disgust.

As I stare hallucinating, it stares back. Stares at me yelling. Yelling, beeping, beeping. Yelling, beeping, yelling.

It's burning, with foul smoke burning. That foul smoke, black charcoal cloud.

Electrons zipping from the clock. Zipping through my brain to disturb my thinking.

Conspiring, yes conspiring!

Standing on the desk; with beeps and boops whispering.

To me whispering.

Agony, hatred, and disgust.

To me whispering.

As I stare hallucinating.
