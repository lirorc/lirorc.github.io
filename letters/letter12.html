The twelfth letter

I am sorry for not writing you any letters for a while.

No, I wasn't busy. It's just that I couldn't think of anything new to write about.

Everything's as usual, you know. I'm fine though, don't worry about me. By the way how is your family, are they well? I know things can get a little rough but go easy on them: I am sure there is no one else that love you as much. Tell them I said hi; tell them I'm all right.

Tell them I'm sorry for what happened.
